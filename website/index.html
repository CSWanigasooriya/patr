Smart AI Companion for Effortless and Personalized Travel Planning.

Travel Genie is your intelligent travel planning assistant powered by cutting-edge AI. Whether you're planning a quick weekend getaway or an extended international adventure, our personalized engine helps you discover, plan, and optimize your trips — just the way you like.

  • Tailored Travel Recommendations
    Receive destination suggestions that align with your interests, travel style, and past preferences.
  • Smart Query Suggestions
    Get helpful autocomplete and natural language prompts while you explore travel options, making planning effortless.
  • Budget-Aware Planning
    Set your budget and let our AI recommend optimal travel options that stay within your spending limits.
  • Weather-Savvy Itineraries
    Get real-time weather insights so your plans adapt to sunshine or storms.
  • Event & Function Awareness
    Discover local festivals, concerts, and events during your trip to make your journey even more exciting.

With Travel Genie, you’re not just booking a trip — you’re crafting an experience.
